The Largest Selection of Wine and Spirits in Mississippi

Cocktails, wine, spirits, and beer are designed to go hand-in-hand with the menu at The Manship. Dining well here means drinking well. Our bar features an extensive list of hand-picked wine that received a Wine Spectator Award of Excellence, craft beer, wildly inventive cocktails, and the largest collection of whiskeys and bourbons in the state.
